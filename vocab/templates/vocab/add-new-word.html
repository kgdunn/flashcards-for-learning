Why? What does this website do?
We use the psychological fact, called the "testing effect", that shows when you are tested you are more likely to remember things. For example, new Dutch words!

How often should you test yourself and how many times should you get the answers correct? Well, this website does that all for you, based on over 30 years of research.

You should test yourself at least once per day, until you get the words correct 3 times in a row.
